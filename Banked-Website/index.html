About the app

Banked is a brand new mobile game that makes saving money for the future fun! The game connects to the users savings account, and rewards the user with in-game points every time they deposit money into this account. These points allow the user to customize their in-game avatar with a range of different clothing, houses, accessories and more! You can also refer a friend and gain more in-game points while also helping someone else!


Our app is designed to help and encourage its users to save money. It makes saving money fun and exciting. Users can set customized goals and targets, and features in the game are unlocked whenever a user reaches a new milestone.



Who is Banked for?

We conducted a survey of our year group, containing questions related to phone usage and spending habits. We found that the majority of people in our course spend at least 4+ hours a day on their phone. We also found that over 60% of our course-mates worry about their savings. At Banked, we set out to make and app that would make saving up money fun and easy, by utilizing our already heavy mobile phone usage in todays society.
